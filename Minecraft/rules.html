Server Rules

Simple guidelines to ensure everyone has a great experience on HermitSMP4.

1

Respect All Players

General Conduct

Treat all members of the HermitSMP community with respect. Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.

2

No Griefing or Stealing

Building & Property

Do not destroy or modify other players' builds without explicit permission. Stealing items from chests, farms, or any player-owned structures is strictly forbidden.

3

Keep Chat Clean & Friendly

Chat

Maintain a positive and constructive atmosphere in chat. Avoid excessive swearing, spamming, or engaging in political/religious debates.

4

No Exploiting Glitches/Bugs

Gameplay

Using any known or discovered game exploits, bugs, or unintended mechanics to gain an unfair advantage is prohibited. Report bugs to staff immediately.

5

Build Responsibly

Building & Property

Ensure your builds are reasonable in size and do not cause significant lag. Avoid building too close to spawn or other players' bases without consent.

6

No AFK Machines

Gameplay

Automated AFK machines or devices designed to keep you online and active without actual player interaction are not allowed.

7

Use Common Sense

General Conduct

Many situations are not explicitly covered by rules. Use your best judgment and act in a way that contributes positively to the server experience.

8

No Advertising

Chat

Do not advertise other Minecraft servers, websites, or products in chat or through builds without prior staff approval.

9

Follow Staff Instructions

General Conduct

Server staff (Admins, Moderators) are here to ensure a fair and fun environment. Their decisions are final and must be respected.

10

Custom Item Usage

Gameplay

Custom items and unique mechanics are part of the server experience. Do not attempt to duplicate, exploit, or misuse them in ways not intended by the server design.
